VR Cinematic Sandbox

The first of three VR projects for FRNT-489 "VR Storytelling". This was made in Unreal 5.2 and 5.3 using assets from the Stylized Fantasy Provencal asset pack on the Epic Games launcher. A Cinematic Sandbox is something where you as the player don't have to do much in order to get the story to play out infront of you. You walk around, maybe move a couple things when told, but it's one of the more passive types of experiences.
